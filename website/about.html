Design



Rattan can be sprinkled throughout many different design styles but is most commonly used in earthy and organic environments such as Bohemian, coastal, organic modern and tropical.

Weaving



Rattan weaving is applied with some similar techniques as bamboo weaving, yet has its own crafts, especially flower braiding technique, rolling skills, interlacing flower braids.

Finishing&Coloring



Rattan is a saturated, shaded, tree branch yellow with an earthy undertone. It is a perfect paint color for an accent wall or a man-cave. Pair it with deep olive or brown for trim.
